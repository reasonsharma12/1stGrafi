#IITR ALUMNI ASSOCIATION

The IITRAA is soul of every Alumni and is universally known to be an
integral part of their lives.
The Roorkee College was established in 1847 AD as the First Engineering
College in the British Empire. The College was renamed as The
Thomason College of Civil Engineering in 1854. It was given the status of
University by Act No. IX of 1948 of the United Province (Uttar Pradesh) in
recognition of its performance and its potential and keeping in view the
needs of post-independent India.

Vision

We endeavor to -
- Make IITRAA the most respected and valued networked
Association committed to meet the aspirations of its
members globally.
- Initiate and implement measures to establish a culture
of efficiency, transparency, seamless integration of
Alumni, regional chapters, and unhindered exchange of
ideas.

Mission

Expand, strengthen and develop a lifelong bond between
IIT Roorkee Alumni globally by exploiting network
opportunities, harnessing the wealth of experience of
diverse backgrounds and potential for mutual benefits of
all age groups. Concurrently, build bridges and strengthen
our relationship with our Alma mater....
